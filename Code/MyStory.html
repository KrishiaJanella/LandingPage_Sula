My Story





Embark on a nostalgic journey through the canvas of my life, where each chapter unfolds a unique tale of childhood wonder, teenage exploration, and the transformative years of college. In the vibrant landscape of my early years, every playtime moment was a vibrant brushstroke of self-discovery. From the innocence of building sandcastles to the excitement of chasing butterflies, my childhood was a kaleidoscope of curiosity and joy.




Childhood Years

My childhood, though a mix of joy, love, and sorrow, is a bit fuzzy in my memory, filled with moments that blur together.1 Born from a teenage pregnancy, my mom was just 18, and my dad, 22, forming a young family with uncertain beginnings.2 They met in college, but a tragic incident separated them four years later, leaving behind unanswered questions about their past.3 I don't know much more about their history because my mom and I didn't talk about it much, perhaps shielding me from painful truths.4 Maybe she just didn't want me to miss my dad, who was taken by a road accident, leaving a void in our lives.5 During the four years when my dad was around, I felt incredibly special, basking in his love and attention.6 We shared a strong bond, and I was a total daddy's girl, cherishing every moment together.7 Even when he got mad at me for being naughty, those are the happy memories I hold onto dearly.8 One particular memory stands out when I was 2 – always asking for a dollhouse , a simple desire amidst financial struggles.9 Money was tight; my dad worked in fast-food joints, and my mom just finished college, making ends meet with difficulty.10 Despite that, my whims were never granted until one day when my dad got creative, showcasing his resourcefulness and love.11 I remember finding him at a table outside our house, using cardboard from a nearby store to create a dollhouse replica of our home, a gesture that touched my heart deeply.12 It might not have been as fancy as store-bought ones, but it was the most beautiful thing to me, a symbol of his love and dedication.13 After my dad unexpectedly passed away before I turned 5, the only thing I remember him giving me is that dollhouse, a precious memento that holds immense sentimental value.14 It's a constant reminder of his love and dedication, going the extra mile with limited resources, leaving a lasting imprint on my heart.15 What makes the dollhouse special is that it's one of a kind, created by my father with the sole purpose of making me happy, a testament to his creativity and love.16 The loss of my father shaped my childhood, and as I grew older, I realized I lost many memories trying to hold onto the moments we had together, a bittersweet journey of remembrance.17 Being a daddy's girl and then losing him was painful, but it molded me into the strong woman I am today, and for that, I'm grateful, carrying his legacy within me. 18 Those memories, though sometimes painful, are the foundation of who I've become, guiding me through life's challenges with resilience and gratitude.19 Amidst the haze of recollection, I find solace in the lessons learned and the love that continues to shape my journey.20

"These cherished photos capture moments from my childhood, each a precious memory from various places my family and I explored together. From delightful visits to Manila Zoo to joyful outings at local malls, these snapshots hold a special place in my heart. Among them, you'll find glimpses of my father, John Yuller, adding an extra layer of warmth and nostalgia to these treasured mementos."




Teenage Years

My high school years were a rollercoaster of events, filled with both laughter and tears.1 It was during this period that I became acutely aware of our financial struggles, with my mother being the sole breadwinner in our family.2 To alleviate the financial burden, I made the decision to enroll in a public school for high school.3 Taytay National High School became my new academic home, and initially, the environment felt overwhelming – hot classrooms and overcrowded spaces, a stark contrast to my private elementary school.4 Despite my initial doubts, as days passed, I realized the invaluable knowledge I gained from excellent teachers at little to no cost.5 I was fortunate to be part of the pilot class, the Special Science Class (SSC), where high-achieving students were grouped together.6 This class brought its challenges with stringent grade requirements, demanding expectations, and additional classes like research, pre-calculus, advanced physics, and statistics.7 Juggling these commitments with school organizations, contests, and events proved challenging, but I navigated through successfully.8 Being part of the SSC not only provided advanced knowledge crucial for my college journey but also played a significant role in shaping my character.9 It cultivated resilience, studiousness, and a strong work ethic.10 Besides my family's support, the class, dedicated teachers, former classmates, and the intense yet memorable environment were instrumental in my success in passing the DOST scholarship exam.11 Witnessing the result was a moment of immense joy and pride.12 Now, with the scholarship, I can contribute to my college expenses without burdening my mother with hefty tuition fees and associated costs.13 My high school experience wasn't just about academic grind; it was a journey of self-improvement driven by the desire to support my family and secure a better future for myself.14 Reflecting on those years fills me with gratitude for the friendships forged, the lessons learned, and the growth experienced.15 Each challenge I faced became an opportunity to strengthen my resolve and determination, preparing me for the journey ahead.16 As I embark on the next chapter of my life, I carry with me the lessons learned and the memories cherished from my high school years.17 Those formative years not only shaped my academic trajectory but also instilled in me a sense of resilience and determination that continues to guide me today.18 The bonds forged during my high school journey remain steadfast, serving as a reminder of the strength found in community and shared aspirations.19 As I look back on those transformative years, I am filled with gratitude for the experiences that have molded me into the person I am today.20

"These photos hold a treasure trove of memories from my unforgettable high school years. They capture moments shared with friends and classmates who remain dear to me even today. Together, we navigated the highs and lows of adolescence, forging bonds that have stood the test of time. These snapshots serve as a poignant reminder of the laughter, challenges, and camaraderie that defined those formative years."




College Years

Starting college was nerve-wracking for me.1 Originally, I had planned to enroll in a public university due to its affordability and my familiarity with the public school environment.2 However, my mother insisted that a private school, specifically UE known for excellence in IT, would offer better opportunities and learning experiences.3 With a scholarship to cover tuition fees, I yielded to my mother's persuasion.4 Being naturally introverted, adapting to a new environment with unfamiliar faces was overwhelming.5 Despite my initial reservations, I worked diligently at the start of the semester, particularly since I had little knowledge about my chosen course, though the field of technology fascinated me.6 Surprisingly, programming, a subject I feared before college, turned out to be enjoyable, albeit challenging at times.7 The semester also introduced me to new friends, adding excitement to my college experience.8 Living far from the university posed its own set of challenges.9 Commuting daily from my residence required waking up at 3:30 in the morning for a 7:30 class, creating stress before the school day even began.10 Days with physical education classes added to the struggle, with the need to carry an extra bag for my PE uniform and rubber shoes.11 Despite these hardships, my efforts paid off, qualifying me for the university scholarship, which, thankfully, allowed me to secure a dormitory.12 The dormitory significantly improved my college life by saving time on commuting and providing a conducive environment for studying and pursuing other interests.13 Achieving this scholarship and the positive changes it brought have brought me genuine happiness.14 As I navigate through college, I aim to make the most of every opportunity that comes my way, embracing challenges and growth with enthusiasm.15 Each step forward brings me closer to my goals, and I'm determined to seize every moment with passion and dedication.16 With a supportive network of friends and mentors by my side, I am confident that I can overcome any obstacles that may arise and thrive in my academic pursuits.17 Here's to embracing the journey ahead and making the most of every experience that comes my way.18 I look forward to continuing these achievements throughout my college journey, and I'm excited for the next chapter in my life.19 Until then, see you in my next adventure.20

"These photos encapsulate who I am today—someone who embraces life to the fullest while navigating the responsibilities of adulthood. Amidst the vibrant scenes of joy and laughter, I am surrounded by cherished friends and classmates who infuse my journey with richness and vitality. Their presence adds depth and meaning to every moment, making this journey through life all the more captivating and fulfilling."
